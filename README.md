# How AI is Disrupting Your Business Model

An independent companion guide to Board of Innovation's webinar on [How AI is Disrupting Your Business Model](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JMf1ELSrqGo), featuring structured analysis of the key frameworks and insights presented.

**Live site:** [tapfumamv.github.io/boi-ai-disruption-webinar](https://tapfumamv.github.io/boi-ai-disruption-webinar/)

## What is this?

A magazine-style editorial breakdown of Board of Innovation's webinar on AI-driven business model disruption. The webinar covers how incumbents lose market share to AI-native competitors, the frameworks behind disruption patterns, and what leaders can do about it.

Six themes distilled across three acts: the problem, the &ldquo;how,&rdquo; and the response.

## Features

- Narrative sections with slide viewer carousels
- Quick Take cards with expand/collapse
- Animated icons (Lordicon)
- Light/dark mode
- Responsive, mobile-first design
- Side-by-side text and slide layout on desktop
